# User Manual: Chord Cards & Handpan Visualizer

## Grid Editor

The **Grid Editor** is a powerful built-in sequencing tool that allows you to create complex melodic and rhythmic patterns with precision. Designed as a professional-grade "piano roll" for handpan players, it provides a visual canvas where you can draw, move, and edit notes across your chosen scale.

### Key Features
- **Precise Sequencing**: Use the grid to place notes at specific time intervals. You can adjust the resolution (1/4 to 1/16 notes, including triplets) to craft everything from simple drones to rapid-fire percussive flourishes.
- **Dynamic Note Editing**: Easily drag and drop notes to change their pitch or timing. The editor supports multi-selection, allowing you to move entire motifs or duplicate complex phrases effortlessly.
- **History Control**: Fully integrated Undo and Redo functionality ensures you can experiment with confidence, knowing you can always backtrack through your creative process.

### Guitar Hero Mode 🎸
One of the most unique features of the Grid Editor is **Guitar Hero Mode**. When activated, the interface transforms into a distraction-free, high-contrast playback environment. 

In this mode, all menus and toolbars are hidden, and the grid expands to fill your entire screen. This provides a clear, focused view of the upcoming notes as they scroll, making it ideal for practice sessions or live performances where visual clarity is paramount. It allows you to focus purely on the flow of the music without any UI clutter getting in the way.

### Integration
Once your masterpiece is ready, the **Export** function instantly converts your grid into a "Phrase" item within your progression. This allows you to combine sequenced patterns with standard chords, creating a seamless musical journey.

## Practice & Performance Controls

### Modular Compositions
The **Progression Stage** is designed for modular learning. Every chord or phrase added to your sequence can be independently toggled:
- **Individual Mute**: Use the speaker icon on each card to enable or disable specific parts of your composition. This is perfect for isolating a melody or practice-playing over a specific harmonic background.
- **Global Mute**: The master mute button in the header allows you to quickly silence all sequenced parts, turning the app into a pure visual reference or a manual practice pad.

### Precount & Timing
To help you get into the rhythm, the app features a flexible **Precount** system:
- **Standard Precount**: Choose from common countdown lengths (3, 4, 6, etc.) to give yourself time to prepare before the music starts.
- **"Each Loop" Mode**: When enabled, the precount will play not only at the start but also before every repetition of the progression, providing a consistent rhythmic anchor for practice.

### Custom Precount: A Learning Tool
The **Custom Precount** (accessible via the `...` button) is a sophisticated tool for advanced rhythmic study:
- **Odd Meters**: Beyond standard 4/4 time, you can set the precount to any number of beats (e.g., 5, 7, or 11) to match complex compositions.
- **Micro-Timing & Accents**: Adjust subdivisions (1/8, 1/16, etc.) and toggle strong/weak accents for each beat. This allows you to create a "custom click track" that perfectly mirrors the internal pulse of the piece you are studying, making it an invaluable tool for deconstructing and mastering intricate handpan techniques.
